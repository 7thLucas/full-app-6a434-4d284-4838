# Navin — Design Guidelines

## Brand personality
Clean, modern, minimal, optimistic, sustainability-forward. Premium consumer-app feel. Evokes clear water and environmental care. Friendly and trustworthy.

## Color palette
- **Primary: blue tones** evoking clear water and sustainability.
  - Primary action / brand: blue-600 (e.g. sky/blue ~ #2563EB / #0284C7 range).
  - Accents: lighter sky blues (sky-400/sky-500) for highlights, gradients, droplet motifs.
  - Backgrounds: airy whites and very light blue-tinted neutrals.
  - Text: deep slate/near-black for body; muted slate for secondary.
- Use blue gradients sparingly for hero/water motifs. Match the generated blue water/droplet logo identity.

## Typography
- Clean, modern sans-serif. Generous, readable sizing.
- Clear hierarchy: bold confident headings, comfortable body text.

## Layout & components
- **Mobile-first** — this is a mobile + web community app; design primarily for phone screens, scale up gracefully to web.
- Rounded cards (generous corner radius), soft shadows, airy spacing.
- Member hub / home dashboard surfacing rewards balance, referral status, and community status as clean cards.
- Bottom nav or simple tab structure suited to a mobile community app.
- Premium, polished consumer-app aesthetic — not corporate/enterprise.

## Imagery & motifs
- Water droplets, gentle waves, subtle sustainability cues.
- Keep it minimal and optimistic — avoid clutter.

## Elevation & feel
- Light, airy elevation with soft blue-tinted shadows.
- Smooth, delightful micro-interactions where appropriate.
