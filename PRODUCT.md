# Navin — Product Information

## What it is
Navin is a community-driven membership app for a sustainability-focused water brand. It turns the brand's customers into a loyal, self-growing community: customers register as members, verify their purchases, invite friends, and earn rewards — driving repeat purchase and word-of-mouth growth around a sustainability value proposition. Web + mobile, but mobile-first.

## The core insight
A consumer water brand lives or dies on repeat purchase and word-of-mouth, not technology novelty. The compounding engine is the membership + referral community core: register → verify purchase → invite friends → earn rewards. Carbon tracking and Web3 are real differentiators, but only matter once the community exists, so they are staged for later and NOT in the launch MVP.

## Launch MVP — BUILD THIS NOW
1. **Member registration** — customers sign up and join the community.
2. **Purchase verification** — validate membership eligibility / reward actions from proof of purchase.
3. **Referral & invitations** — members invite friends via a personal referral link; both sides earn rewards.
4. **Loyalty & rewards** — points/incentives for sustainable consumption and community growth.
5. **Engagement home** — a member hub surfacing rewards, referrals, and community status.

## DO NOT BUILD — staged/future stubs only
- Water-consumption & carbon-impact tracking
- Tokenized / Web3 rewards
- Health & insurance ecosystem integrations
Keep these visible as "coming soon" / staged future goals so the brand story stays whole, but do not implement them.

## Target users
Urban, health-conscious, digital-native professionals in Indonesia who care about sustainability. Comfortable with subscription and digital platforms, active-lifestyle.

## Brand & tone
- Product name: **Navin**.
- Clean, minimal blue water/droplet identity.
- Tone: clean, modern, optimistic, sustainability-forward.

## Strategic principles
- Launch on the community flywheel (membership + referral), not on Web3.
- Keep the full sustainability/Web3 vision visible as staged goals.
- Verified, real actions (purchases, referrals) are the operations that matter — not signups or page views alone.
